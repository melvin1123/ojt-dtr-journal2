## Overview

The **Weekly Journal Report** allows interns to document their activities, learning progress, outputs, and reflections during the internship. Reports are submitted regularly and reviewed by an administrator.  

Certified reports are finalized and intended to remain unchanged.  

---

## Accessing the Weekly Journal

1. Log in to the **Intern Panel**.  
2. Navigate to **Weekly Journal Reports**.  
3. You will see a list of your submitted reports with their current status:  
   - **Pending**  
   - **Viewed**  
   - **Certified**  

---

## Creating a Weekly Journal Report

### Steps

1. Click **New weekly reports**.  
2. Fill in all required sections: 
   - `Week Range`
   - `Week Focus`  
   - `Topics and Concepts Learned`  
   - `Outputs and Links(REQUIRED)`  
   - `What you built or designed*`  
   - `Decisions & Reasoning`  
   - `Challenges / Blockers`  
   - `What youd improve next time`  
   - `Key Takeaway of the week`
   - `Signature` 
3. Review your answers carefully.  
4. Click **Create**.  

### Notes

- All fields are required.  
- Multiple topics and outputs can be added.  
- After submission, the report status will be **Pending**.  

---

## Viewing a Weekly Journal Report

### Steps

1. Go to **Weekly Journal Reports**.  
2. Click on a report to view its contents.  
3. You can see:  
   - Week coverage  
   - Report status  
   - Full journal details  

### Certified Reports

- Certified reports are displayed in **read-only mode**.  
- Editing is disabled for certified reports.  

---

## Updating a Weekly Journal Report

### When Editing Is Allowed

Reports can be updated if their status is:  
- **Pending**  
- **Viewed**  

### When Editing Is Restricted

- Reports marked as **Certified** cannot be edited.  

### Steps

1. Open the report you want to update.  
2. Click **Edit**.  
3. Modify the necessary fields.  
4. Click **Save**.  

### Notes

- All sections must remain filled.  
- The report structure cannot be changed.  
- Changes are saved immediately after submission.  

---

## Report Status Meaning

| Status    | Description                       |
|----------|-----------------------------------|
| Pending  | Submitted and waiting for review  |
| Viewed   | Reviewed by an administrator      |
| Certified| Approved and finalized (read-only)|

---

## Important Reminders

- Ensure your entries are complete and accurate before submission.  
- Certified reports are considered final for evaluation.  
- Reports are used for monitoring internship progress and documenting learning outcomes.  

---

## Summary

The Weekly Journal Report system helps interns track and reflect on progress throughout the internship. Reports are structured, easy to fill out, and reviewed to support your learning experience.
